How We Help You:
LivGood handles the complexities of finding your ideal home, so you can focus on settling into your new life in Canada with peace of mind!

  • We establish a personalized list of housing options for you
  • We consider factors pertinent to you; proximity, price, suitability.
  • We pair you with landlords that align with your needs

  • Accompaniment

At Livgood, we understand that settling into a new country involves more than just finding housing. When newcomers arrive in Canada, they are confronted with a variety of initial tasks that can be overwhelming. That's why our accompaniment service is tailored to ease this transition. We offer personalized assistance with essential tasks to ensure you feel supported right from the start.

Our Accompaniment service includes:

  • Assistance with opening a bank account
  • Help with purchasing initial groceries (value of up to $80)
  • Support in obtaining a SIM card for communication
  • Guidance on acquiring a SIN card for employment and government services

Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities. Book your accompaniment service
